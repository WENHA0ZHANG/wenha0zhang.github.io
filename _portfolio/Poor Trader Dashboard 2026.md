This is a web-based dashboard built because the poor author cannot afford paid data feeds, so he scrape free indicators and stitch them into one panel. It tracks US High Yield OAS, AAII bull-bear spread, CNN Fear & Greed, put/call ratio, S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 P/E, RSI, VIX, and Nasdaq 100 % above the 20-day moving average. Try the live demo here: [Poor Trader Dashboard](https://poor-trader-dashboard.onrender.com) (but first-time opening may take 1–2 minutes to load...), and view the GitHub Repositories: [WENHA0ZHANG/poor-trader-dashboard](https://github.com/WENHA0ZHANG/poor-trader-dashboard).
