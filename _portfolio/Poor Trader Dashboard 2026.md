This is a web-based dashboard built because I cannot afford paid data feeds, so I scrape free indicators and stitch them into one panel. It tracks US High Yield OAS, AAII bull-bear spread, CNN Fear & Greed, put/call ratio, S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 P/E, RSI, VIX, and Nasdaq 100 % above the 20-day moving average.
